Peace is Green '21

WORLD’S LONGEST & LARGEST ENVIRONMENT ONLINE EVENT

24 Hours – A Million Reach

“Sustainability”, “Climate Change”, “Environment” – the words are being searched on google more than ever before. The reason is obvious. We are headed to where search for “Life” will become a struggle if we do not mend our ways and Act Now! But then, this is Us, this is our Life, and we will “Right” it! This is our moment. We cannot turn back time. But we can grow trees, green our cities, rewild our gardens, change our diets and clean up rivers and coasts. We are the generation that can make peace with nature.

To celebrate World Environment Day, Act Now, together with Environment Online, Treebuddy.Earth, Brookfield International School, Glamanand, and Envirate, brings to you, 2nd edition of our web symposium “PEACE IS GREEN-EVERY ACT MATTERS”, world’s longest and largest environment online event, non-stop for 24 hours on 4-5 June 2021. The Web Symposium brings together individuals and organizations, the thinkers and the doers, from around the world, and, may reach more a million participants in over 100 countries!

We invite you to share your climate actions, and let us learn from you. CONTRIBUTE YOUR ACT, EVERY ACT MATTERS!

Let’s get active, not anxious. Let’s reimagine, recreate, restore. Let’s be bold, not timid.

Join Us in #GenerationRestoration
